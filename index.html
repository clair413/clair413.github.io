ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Christina, a senior journalism and mass communication major at Bob Jones University.

company in the southern New Hampshire region. With experience from two
communications internships in the past year and a half, I'm ready to
jump into any company and get started! My time as editor of my
university's newspaper, The Collegian, has also provided me with the opportunity to hone my writing and editing skills in the media world.

I'm a strong writer, editor and content creator with experience in a
variety of subject areas. In the corporate world, I've written high tech
informational articles, nonprofit blog posts, visitor brochures and
social media posts. In the media, I've written news and entertainment,
editorials and opinion columns.

 I also have corporate experience in content marketing, internal/external communications and design with a UX focus.

A hobbyist photographer and videographer, I'm usually found outside exploring nature and capturing its natural beauty. 

My Professional Goals

A common interview question is what you're looking for in a company or in
a specific position. And it's an important topic to consider. My
professional goal is threefold. I hope to work at a company or
organization where I love what I do, why I do it and who I do it with.

So what does this look like for me? It looks like a company where I'm actively communicating with an audience through content creation. It looks like working for a company that cares----cares for its employees, cares for its customers and cares for the surrounding community. And last, it looks like working with a team of awesome people who I enjoy working with.
